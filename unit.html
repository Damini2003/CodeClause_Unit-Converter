A unit is a unit of measurement for a quantity that is established by custom or legislation. A multiple of the unit can be used to represent other amounts. Different unit systems were created and used throughout human history in diverse places and civilizations. The International System of Units (SI), a contemporary version of the metric system, serves as the current standard of measurement for the entire world. Despite the fact that SI is meant for universal usage, it has not yet been fully embraced and several other measurement systems are still in use in some parts of the world.This website aims to provide a simple overview of the many measuring units used in various systems as well as an easy way to convert between them.
